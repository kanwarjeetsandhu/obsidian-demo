System conceived by Tiago Forte

# Projects

- Short collections of tasks or notes
- Collecting information in a folder

# Areas

- Something you're responsible for over a long period of time
- Ongoing engagement or responsibility
- Example: House
	- Responsible for maintenance
	- Making sure the roof doesn't leak
	- Regular painting

# Resources

- Collection of information you expect to be useful in the future
- Reference things you expect to come back to

# Archive

- Notes which are not useful currently or in the near future, but which you don't want to throw away either
